Love and happy Food

We invite you to embark on a culinary adventure with us, celebrating the flavors, tradition, and warmth of Bihar. Our passion for Bihari cuisine is evident in every dish.

We serve, and we can't wait to share the enchantmnet of Champaran Handi Mutton and Chicken with you. Come experience thr true essence of Bihar, one exquisite bite at a time.

Champaran meat, also known as ahuna, handi meat or batlohi, is a dish with its root from Champaran, a district of Bihar. Meat is marinated in a mix of mustard oil and ghee, garlic, onions, ginger with the paste of spices. The mouth of the handi (earthenware pot) is sealed with kneaded flour. It is cooked slowly on a low flame of a wood fire and tossed continuously while cooking. The taste and cooking Best in india
